--- POST 5 | LINKEDIN | Educational ---
Most CRM setups I audit have the same 3 problems — and they're all costing leads.

After reviewing dozens of GoHighLevel accounts, here's what I see consistently:

1. No automated follow-up sequence
   The lead comes in, someone manually reaches out (eventually), and half the time it's too late. Speed-to-lead is everything. Automate it.

2. Pipelines that are just storage, not systems
   A pipeline should tell you exactly what to do next with every contact. If yours is just a list of names, it's not working for you.

3. No re-engagement campaign for cold leads
   The gold is in the follow-up. Most businesses abandon leads after 2–3 touches. A well-built automation can revive months-old leads — automatically.

Getting these three right can meaningfully change your close rate — without adding a single new lead to your pipeline.
